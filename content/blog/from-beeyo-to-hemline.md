## A New Chapter Begins

We are thrilled to announce that **Beeyo** has rebranded to **Hemline** — a name that reflects our commitment to helping fashion designers craft every stitch with precision and creativity.

## Why the Change?

The word "hemline" is iconic in fashion — it's the finishing touch that defines a garment's character. Similarly, Hemline is the AI companion that helps you perfect every detail of your creative vision.

## What This Means for You

- **Same team, same mission** — We are still dedicated to building the best AI workspace for fashion designers
- **All your projects and data remain safe** — Nothing has changed except our name
- **New features coming soon** — Stay tuned for exciting updates to the platform

## A Heartfelt Thank You

To our early users and community: thank you for being part of this journey. Your feedback has shaped Hemline into what it is today, and your trust drives us forward.

We can't wait to continue building the future of fashion design together.
